Hand Sign Recognition

In this project, I delve into real-time hand gesture recognition using Python. Leveraging OpenCV, I capture live video streams from a webcam, and then employ a pre-trained deep learning model (possibly created with Google's Teachable Machine) to identify hand signs ranging from 0 to 5. The goal is to enhance user interaction and accessibility through intuitive gesture-based controls.
